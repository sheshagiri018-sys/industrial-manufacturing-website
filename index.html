Our Story

BUILT IN
SALEM
SINCE 2000

Sri Durga Engineering Works was established in the year 2000 from Salem, Tamil Nadu — one of India's most prominent manufacturing hubs. For over two and a half decades, we have been a dedicated manufacturer of precision roll forming machinery serving the construction, solar, and industrial sectors across India.

Our product range spans 8 machine categories with over 70 machine variants, covering everything from basic corrugated roofing sheet machines to fully automatic multi-profile systems and solar mounting structure forming lines. Every machine is built to exacting tolerances and dispatched with full after-sales support.

We operate as a GST-registered proprietorship with complete business transparency. Factory-direct sales mean no middlemen — you deal directly with the manufacturer, getting the best price and the most accurate technical guidance.

How We Build

MANUFACTURING
PROCESS

Every machine passes through 7 rigorous stages before leaving our Salem facility.

Step 01
Design & Engineering
Custom machine design based on the required roll forming profile and customer specifications.
Step 02
Material Sourcing
Quality steel, gears, motors and electronic components sourced from verified suppliers.
Step 03
Roll Tool Machining
Precision CNC machining of forming rollers to exact profile tolerances. Heat treated for hardness.
Step 04
Machine Assembly
Complete mechanical assembly of frame, roll stands, gear drive, decoiler and hydraulic systems.
Step 05
Electrical & PLC
PLC control system, motor drives, length encoder and electrical panel integration and wiring.
Step 06
Quality Testing
Full production run test with actual steel coil. Profile dimensions verified, speed calibrated, cutter adjusted.
Step 07
Dispatch & Support
Carefully packed and dispatched from Salem. Installation guidance and after-sales technical support provided.
